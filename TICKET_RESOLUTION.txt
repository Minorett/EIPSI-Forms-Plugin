Why flex: 0 1 auto is better than flex: 1:

  flex: 1 = flex: 1 1 0px
    โ grow: YES (expand to fill space)
    โ shrink: YES (shrink if needed)
    โ basis: 0px (start from 0, divide space equally)
    โ Result: Equal distribution, ignores content size
    
  flex: 0 1 auto
    โ grow: NO (don't expand unnecessarily)
    โ shrink: YES (shrink only if needed)
    โ basis: auto (use natural content size)
    โ Result: Respects content, flexible layout

Why overflow: visible matters:

  overflow: hidden
    โ Clips content that extends beyond container
    โ Forces text truncation
    โ Adds artificial "..." with text-overflow
    โ BAD: Confuses users
    
  overflow: visible
    โ Shows content completely
    โ Allows flexible positioning
    โ No artificial truncation
    โ GOOD: User sees full text
